Features


  • VoIPFlare provides phone line rental services using two options: either through BT or other VoIP providers. These phone lines are connected to the VoIPFlare Network, allowing users to make and receive calls over the internet.

  • If you choose our BT Line rental option, it will cost more due to the fees we have to pay to BT for using their lines. In addition to a setup fee, we also have to purchase equipment to convert the analog signal to VoIP.

  • On the other hand, our internet line option is less expensive since we can secure telephone lines at a lower cost than BT. Setup fees are usually around £1, and there is no need to purchase additional equipment as the line is already online.
