What We Do


    ___

  • At VoIPFlare, we handle the process of purchasing and arranging for the installation of a BT line to our office. The installation typically takes around 14 days. While we wait for the installation to be completed, we take care of acquiring an analog to VoIP converter, which also takes approximately 14 days. Once we receive the converter, we connect it to the BT line and integrate it with our your network for Voice communication purposes. This allows you to seamlessly transition to VoIP services and enjoy the benefits of improved call quality, and advanced features.
